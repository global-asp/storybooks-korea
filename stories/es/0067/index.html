Cocinando 요리 Cooking

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Kathy Arbuckle

Karina Vásquez

Áurea Vericat

Spanish

Level 1

Pelo las papas.

난 감자를 벗겨.

I peel the potatoes.

Corto el repollo.

난 양배추를 썰어.

I chop a cabbage.

Rallo las zanahorias.

난 당근을 갈아.

I grate the carrots.

Lavo los frijoles.

난 콩을 씻어.

I wash the beans.

Corto la calabaza.

난 버터넛을 잘라.

I cut the butternut.

Pico la espinaca.

난 시금치를 썰어.

I chop the spinach.

Mi mamá trocea las cebollas.

우리 엄마는 양파를 썰으셔.

My mom chops the onions.

Las cebollas me hacen llorar cuando las cortan.

양파는 썰렸을 때 날 울게 해.

Onions make me cry when they are chopped.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Kathy Arbuckle
Translated by: Karina Vásquez
Read by: Áurea Vericat
Language: Spanish
Level 1
