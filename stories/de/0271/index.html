Zwei 둘 Two

Carole Bloch

Richard MacIntosh

Anna Westpfahl

Jula Eberth

German

Level 1

Zwei kleine Hände zum Halten.

잡을 수 있는 두 작은 손.

Two little hands to hold.

Zwei kleine Füße zum Schießen.

찰 수 있는 두 작은 발.

Two little feet to kick.

Zwei kleine Augen zum Sehen.

볼 수 있는 두 작은 눈.

Two little eyes to see.

Zwei kleine Ohren zum Hören.

들을 수 있는 두 작은 귀.

Two little ears to hear.

Und zwei liebende Arme zum Umarmen.

그리고 안을 수 있는 두 작은 팔!

And two loving arms to HUG!

Written by: Carole Bloch
Illustrated by: Richard MacIntosh
Translated by: Anna Westpfahl
Read by: Jula Eberth
Language: German
Level 1
Source: Two from African Storybook
