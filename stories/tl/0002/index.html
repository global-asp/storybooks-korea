Pagmasdan ang mga hayop 이 동물들을 보세요 Look at the animals

Jenny Katz

Sandy Campbell

Karla Comanda

La Trinidad Mina

Tagalog

Level 1

Pagmasdan ang mga hayop.

이 동물들을 보세요.

Look at the animals.

“Unga,” sabi ng baka.

소는 "음메" 라고 해요.

The cow says, "Moo."

“Meh, meh,” sabi ng kambing.

염소는 "메헤헤헤, 메헤헤."

The goat says, "Meh, meh."

“Hing,” sabi ng kabayo.

말은 "히히히힝."

The horse says, "Neigh."

“Igik, igik,” sabi ng baboy.

돼지는 "꿀꿀."

The pig says, "Grunt."

“Tik-ti-la-ok,” sabi ng manok.

닭은 "꼬꼬."

The chicken says, "Cluck."

“Aw, aw,” sabi ng aso.

개는 "멍멍."

The dog says, "Woof."

“Shhh!” sabi ng magsasaka.

농부는 "쉿!" 이라 말해요.

The farmer says, "Shhh!"
