Apoy 불 Fire

Deborah Namugosa, Beatrice Nabune, Alison Tukei, Rose Sabano

Rob Owen

Karla Comanda

La Trinidad Mina

Tagalog

Level 1

Tingnan mo, may apoy!

불을 보세요!

Look, fire!

Nakasusunog ang apoy.

불은 태워요.

Fire burns.

Nakaluluto ang apoy.

불은 요리도 해요.

Fire cooks.

Nagbibigay-init ang apoy.

불은 따뜻하게 해줘요.

Fire gives warmth.

Nagbibigay-liwanag ang apoy.

불은 밝게 해줘요.

Fire gives light.

Tingnan mo, may apoy!

불을 보세요!

Look, fire!

Kamangha-mangha ang apoy.

불은 대단해요.

Fire is wonderful.

At makapangyarihan ito.

그리고 강하지요.

And it is powerful.

Written by: Deborah Namugosa, Beatrice Nabune, Alison Tukei, Rose Sabano
Illustrated by: Rob Owen
Translated by: Karla Comanda
Read by: La Trinidad Mina
Language: Tagalog
Level 1
