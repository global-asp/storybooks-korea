Nasaan ang pusa ko? 내 고양이는 어디있지? Where is my cat?

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Bronwen Heath, Ingrid Schechter

Arlene Avila

La Trinidad Mina

Tagalog

Level 1

Nasaan ang pusa ko?

내 고양이는 어디있지?

Where is my cat?

Nasa ilalim ba ng kama?

침대 밑에 있나?

Is it under the bed?

Nasa ibabaw ba ng cabinet?

찬장 위에 있나?

Is it on top of the cupboard?

Nasa likod ba ng sofa?

소파 뒤에 있나?

Is it behind the couch?

Nasa tabi ba ng basurahan?

쓰레기통 옆에 있나?

Is it next to the bin?

Nasa loob ba ng basket?

바구니 안에 있나?

Is it inside the basket?

O sa labas ng bahay?

집 밖에 있나?

Is it outside the house?

Andito lang pala!

여기 있구나!

Here it is!

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Bronwen Heath, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Arlene Avila
Read by: La Trinidad Mina
Language: Tagalog
Level 1
